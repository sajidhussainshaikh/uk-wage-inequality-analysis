# UK Wage Inequality Analysis 
## AI, Automation and the Divergence of UK Sectoral Pay – Evidence from ONS Data 2025 

### Overview
This project analyses UK sectoral wage growth and inequality using the Annual Survey of Hours and Earnings (ASHE) published by the Office of National Statistics (ONS). 

The central question: are sectors with higher AI and technology adoption seeing stronger wage growth than sectors dependent on physical labour? 

### Key Findings 
- Only 1 in 5 broad sectors saw real wage growth between 2010 and 2025
- The highest wage growth sectors - computers, financial services, advertising – correlate with high AI adoption
- The lowest wage growth sectors – catering, retail, manufacturing of physical goods – correlate with low AI adoption
- The gap between the highest and lowest paid sectors in 7.5x
